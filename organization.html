KOICA

Mission: Cooperation and mutual development for peace and prosperity by focusing on people.

Vision: Representative Organization of Korea for Development Cooperation to Implement Global Social Values. Above you can find, KOICA's ongoing projects, in cooperation with different Peruvian entities. Always thinking about the 4 Ps (Person, Prosperity, Peace and Planet) and KOICA's motto "Leave no one behind", in development cooperation.

Ongoing projects


Emergency assistance project for covid-19 response in the Peruvian Amazon (30 November 2020 as of December 31, 2021) $1,000,000.00 USD Objective: To provide necessary medical equipment including ambulances (river and land) as well as to carry out a regular training course for capacity building in relation to the operation and maintenance of equipment, contribute to the strengthening of health services for timely and quality care for patients with covid-19 and other diseases, mitigate the effects of covid-19 on the health of communities in the project area Partners: MINSA and GORE Loreto

Project for Invigorating ICT-based innovation and entrepreneurship (2020-2024) $8,000,000 USD Objective: To strengthen ICT-based innovation and entrepreneurship and competitiveness in Lima, Arequipa and La Libertad through the improvement of innovation capacity and its system in Peru Partners: National Council of Science and Technology – CONCYTEC
