introduction


Korea International Cooperation Agency (KOICA) was founded in April 1991 as a government-funded agency dedicated to providing aid programs. KOICA strives to combat poverty and support the sustainable socio-economic growth of developing countries. The Government of the Republic of Korea, through KOICA, opened its office in Lima in October 2000 and implemented 3 different technical cooperation programs that are Projects, Volunteer Program and CIAT Scholarship Program, on topics such as Rural Development, Health, Education, Information and Communication Technologies.

Concytec is the governing institution of the National System of Science and Technology and Technological Innovation, SINACYT, made up of the Academy, the State Research Institutes, business organizations, communities and civil society. It is governed by the Framework Law of Science and Technology No. 28303. It presents different units and actions for the promotion of STI, such as the PeruCris platform, which will strengthen the generation of information on the science, technology and innovation system for decision making, as well as for the monitoring and control of actions.

Ideals of Inha :Development of Character, Search for the Truth, and Service to Society Founded in 1954 to fulfill the dearest wish of empowering and bringing prosperity to the nation, Inha University stands for the Ideals of building students' characters, searching for the truth and serving society in order to embody the University's founding principles of creativity, diligence and service.

Gyeonggi Center for Creative Economy & Innovation (GCCEI) supports startups in ICT sector, such as 5G, AI, and IoT, to lead future industries. * Center for Creative Economy & Innovation (CCEI) is operating 19 regional centers in Korea to establish public-private partnership (PPP) with local government and corporate partner. As a main figure of local startup hub, CCEI performs as moderator for each regional startup ecosystem.
