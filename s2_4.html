Inha Univ

About Inha


Ideals of Inha :Development of Character, Search for the Truth, and Service to Society
Founded in 1954 to fulfill the dearest wish of empowering and bringing prosperity to the nation, Inha University stands for the Ideals of building students' characters, searching for the truth and serving society in order to embody the University's founding principles of creativity, diligence and service.

“Truth” the school’s motto, indicates we as a research university endeavor to contribute to improving the nation as well as all human society, to embark on great ventures into the world, and to attain academic achievements in our quest for truth through continuous education and training. On the basis of the above ideals, we strive to attain the following goals to build character in our students, foster healthy ideas, and achieve our educational objective of cultivating talented individuals who will contribute to the development of the nation and human society by studying profound theories and applying them in reality.


Our goals

  • To nurture talented individuals who contribute to the betterment of humanity based on ethical conduct and critical judgement


  • To foster talented individuals who possess creativity and the ability to rise to the challenge with insight into global trends, while pursuing practical knowledge and truth


  • To nurture talented individuals who possess the skills to lead the nation and pursue prosperity for its people

About Inha CIDCI

(Center for International Development Cooperation & Innovation)

Inha CIDCI was established in March 2020 to contribute to the prosperity and human development of cooperative partners by discovering innovative human resources development and development cooperation models.

VISION & MISSION

  • The center's mission is to develop innovative human resources and development cooperation models in global development cooperation. Through this, we hope to contribute to the prosperity and human development of developing countries and to establish a platform for international development cooperation.


  • Discover participatory development cooperation models and transfer social innovation know-how to pursue sustainable development based on voluntary development motivation and potential for improvement in developing countries.


  • Establishing a human resources development system in developing countries and expanding the possibility of operating a global joint school system through a cooperative system between Inha University and universities in partner countries.


  • Through this, we aim to enhance the status of internationalization of Inha university and strengthen the financial contribution of university and students' global capabilities by participating in cooperative projects.
