GCCEI


About GCCEI

Center for Creative Economy & Innovation (CCEI) is operating 19 regional centers in Korea to establish public-private partnership (PPP) with local government and corporate partner. As a main figure of local startup hub, CCEI performs as moderator for each regional startup ecosystem.

VISION & MISSION


Key Role
Create local startup ecosystem

Initiate Open Innovation program to support corporate-startup collaboration

Raise investment fund and operate as accelerator



Field of Industry


Create local startup ecosystem

ICT (AI/Big Data, AR/VR, 5G, IoT, Robotics)


Partners


Ministry of SMEs and Startups, KT, Gyeonggi Province, Gyeonggido Business & Science Accelerator, Gyeonggi Content Agency, KOTRA,
National IT Industry Promotion Agency, ASEAN-Korea Centre, Agency for Defense Development, public organizations in overseas, etc.


Key Performance
(As of December, 2020) Incubated 560 startup companies,

Achieved sale revenue of $575.8 million (\626.5 billion),

Created new jobs for 3,039 people, Raised investment of $375.3 million (\408.4 billion)



Main Programs


Accelerating program in full scale : Pre/Early Startup Package Program, K-Champ Challenge, Pangyo Startup Zone

Corporate-startup Collaboration Program with KT : K-Champ Collaboration
Global Hub for Startup Ecosystem : K-Startup Grand Challenge, Global Star Venture Platform, ASEAN-Korea ICT Startup Programme, ODA projects
