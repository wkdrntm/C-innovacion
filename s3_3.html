Universidad Católica San Pablo (UCSP)



San Pablo Catholic University (UCSP), in Arequipa, has approximately 8000 students, distributed in twelve professional careers and nine master's degrees. In its few years of existence, UCSP has established itself as one of the best universities in southern Peru. This is why nine out of ten graduates are working in various companies and public and private institutions. The university promotes the integral formation of the person through activities such as research, teaching, and projection to society. At UCSP, research is an essential task and part of the engine of university life, which nurtures undergraduate and graduate training, and is a strong projection to the community.

The university's vision is "We will be a mature Catholic university in its identity, consolidated as a community, passionate about the search for truth, with a comprehensive training model of high academic level and a protagonist of the development of the region and the country."


San Pablo Catholic University (UCSP) is an academic community animated by the orientations and life of the Catholic Church which, in the light of faith and with the effort of reason, seeks the truth and promotes the integral formation of the human being through activities such as research, teaching, and extension, to contribute to the configuration of culture according to the identity and deployment of the person as a human being.

The Kaman Business Incubator, created in 2015, aims to promote the culture of Entrepreneurship and Innovation that contributes to the development of business initiatives. With the support of international institutions such as the United Nations High Commissioner for Refugees (UNHCR), the Hanns-Seidel-Stiftung organization (Germany), and the Swiss Entrepreneurship Program (Swiss EP); developing entrepreneurship programs, training and events focused on Entrepreneurs, Managers, and Investors that allow the development of the necessary conditions to strengthen the innovation ecosystem.

In these years, Kaman has supported around 150 entrepreneurial projects, 30 of which have managed to raise more than 2 million soles in seed capital for their development. Likewise, the UCSP has developed research projects with the incentives of Law 30309 benefiting companies that research with our university.

In the same way, our university through the Office of Projects and Research Transfer (OPRT) is responsible for advising on the formulation of projects, monitoring, and control of research, development, and innovation projects (R + D + i), being with external or internal financing, of the different academic units of the UCSP. In this way, the OPTI assumes the challenge of promoting the transfer of the new knowledge generated to companies and society in general, seeking to generate positive economic and social effects with the common good as the ultimate goal.

In this sense, in recent years the UCSP has financed more than 40 research projects with its funds, and more than 40 financed by other institutions such as Concytec, Innóvate Peru, and the European Union. Besides, more than 350,000 soles were invested in 134 research trips to national and international destinations, including Chile, Brazil, Italy, France, the United States, Mexico, and China. This strengthens investigations by creating collaborative networks.

Currently our University is included in the QS - LATAM ranking (with position 351-400), and this year 2021 we continue to award important external funds :



  • 2 Fondecyt projects : ~ 2.4 million soles.
  • 3 registration of PCT patents projects : ~ 100 thousand soles.
  • Other research projects : ~ 200 thousand soles.
  • Start of the execution of the international project CODICIS (~ 834 thousand euros.)
