UNI PRESENTATION



The National Engineering University is an educational institution which was founded in 1876 by the Polish engineer Eduardo de Habich, with the name of the Special School of Civil Construction and Mines of Peru, traditionally known as the School of Engineering and turned into a university in 1955 at the NATIONAL UNIVERSITY OF ENGINEERING (UNI). Today it is the first and main training center for engineers, architects and scientists in our country.

The emergence of the School with the specialties of mining engineering and civil engineering is due, as indicated in its creation act, to "the needs of the country."

The UNI reaffirms its commitment to contribute to training people capable of feeling, valuing, interpreting and transforming the world of individual existence, as well as the social collective.

Its formative purposes are reconciled with the search for social justice and the defense of freedom. The scientific and technological culture that the UNI advocates does not pretend to legitimize itself, it aims to satisfy the basic needs of the peoples of Peru. It is respectful of our cultural and biological diversity, and seeks to ensure for society, present and future, the permanence of renewable wealth and the environmental conditions required for its survival and development.

Our University, being a polytechnic, is a university community open to the knowledge and ideas that are discussed and developed in the world, with no other restriction than its ascription to the defense of human rights, peace and well-being.

In the current circumstances of Peru and the World, it is necessary, strategically, to reaffirm its purposes by adapting its actions to the profound changes and challenges of our society.



Mission

"To train leading professionals in science, engineering and architecture in a humanistic way and focused on scientific research, the creation and development of technologies, committed to the continuous improvement of quality and social responsibility, contributing to the sustainable development of the country."

Vision

"Academic institution with accredited professional careers and internationally recognized for its high quality in scientific research and technological innovation, committed to entrepreneurship, the competitiveness of human capital in the public and private spheres, with social responsibility, sustainable development and commitment to the country. "



Motto

"UNI, Science and Technology at the service of the Country"



Infrastructure

UNI has eleven (11) Faculties and more than forty (40) research laboratories. This for the development, integration and application of technology in projects that provide solutions to various problems. It has the capabilities to integrate all technical aspects into any product and meet standards.

ORGANIZATION CHART


Organization chart includes the technology transfer office and the business incubation office with their respective roles within the university.




Innovation Management and Technology Transfer Office - OGIT

Support body for the Vice-Rector's Office for Research, responsible for managing and promoting technological innovation and responsible for designing, executing and monitoring the technology transfer activities of the products obtained in the research carried out by the University. Support research groups in the registration of patents, and identify technological needs in the public and private sectors of the country, promote their innovation and the collection, through bibliometrics and other instruments, of information on the resulting publications of investigations. Likewise, he is in charge of the management of the technology-based incubator Startup UNI. We have 4 work areas :


  • Innovation and Technology Transfer

    • It promotes technological innovation and technology transfer activities for the products developed by the University.

  • UNI-Company link

    • It promotes technological innovation and technology transfer activities for the products developed by the University.

  • Patent Management and Support

    • Promotes and supports the researcher in the management of patent registration and intellectual property rights in all its forms

  • Innovation ventures

    • Manages and promotes innovation ventures and the technology-based business incubator, Startup UNI.



Startup UNI Business Incubator

Startup UNI is the technology-based business incubator that seeks to accompany entrepreneurs to conduct business from technology through Pre-incubation, Incubation and Specialized Courses programs, in addition to the help of our strategic allies in acceleration programs and funds of Seed capital.
We seek to accompany the entrepreneur on this learning route for which we have 5 stations :


  • Entrepreneurs Club :

    • student organization that seeks to generate business skills and competencies in UNI students, generate a network of contacts and constantly train them.

  • Pre-Incubation Program :

    • In a period of 3 months, the entrepreneur will receive training and personalized advice that will allow to validate the business model, create their MVP, generate a network of contacts and also the possibility of being one of the applicants to the C-Innovation program.

  • C-Innovation

    • This program is carried out in conjunction with the KOICA cooperation of Korea where teachers from this country will be able to train entrepreneurs and give a deeper validation to their business model, generate a network of contacts with Peruvian and Korean startups, receive mentoring by professionals Peruvians and foreigners.

  • Incubation Program :

    • It is aimed at enterprises that already have a validated business model. It has a duration of 6 months where the enterprises will receive training and mentoring for the validation of their prototype, it will help to prepare the marketing and finance plan, and prepare them so that they can expose in front of investors and present themselves to competitive funds.

  • Accelerating Digital Transformation Program :

    • The venture that has an outstanding evaluation at the end of the Incubation program may have the opportunity to apply. This is a program where experts in technological ventures will be able to accompany them in this stage through workshops and mentoring, it will be possible to generate a network of contacts with foreign entrepreneurs and the possibility of receiving seed capital.
