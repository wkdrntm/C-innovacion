UNSA

Information of the affiliated University



Mission

train competent and ethical professionals with the capacity for research and innovation, generating scientific, technological and humanistic knowledge, contributing to the sustainable development of the region and the country.

Vision

highly creative and innovative university that practices and participates in democracy and, that is recognized worldwide as a model for the creation and distribution of knowledge. Being a partner of the natural environment, educator of highly competent citizens and defenders of critical thinking, generator and platform of scientific and technological education for the society of the future.





At the San Agustin National University of Arequipa, it is guaranteed that all Agustinian students have the opportunity to access a quality educational service, which is possible through continuous improvement of the teaching-learning, research and social responsibility. The result is the comprehensive training of competent professionals with values, who manage to exceed the expectations of stakeholders and society.



institutional strategic objectives

Improve the quality of the professional training for the university students and the quality of the skills for the university professors. Taking as main actions the modernization, contextualization and articulation to the job market and incorporating formative research and the development of a vision of social responsibility of students inside and outside the University. In the same way, mechanisms are generated in the Graduate School for the completion of a Master and Doctorate studies of practising professors.

Carry out and promote scientific, technological and humanistic research in the academic community. The main purpose is to promote the development of research projects in the academic community, the strengthening of analytic capacities in professors and the equipping of state-of-the-art research centres as research hotbeds for students. Likewise, it seeks to encourage the publication of intellectual production in specialized magazines and the transfer of scientific and technological innovations, as well as their application at the experimental production centres of the University; having as support a program of research funds and fundraising for the sustainable development of research at the University.

Strengthen the social extension and projection for the academic community. An approach that is assumed and incorporated integrally in all the internal activities of the University, like those in academic, research, community well-being, administrative management activities and those linked with its environment. Besides, a volunteer spirit is promoted among students.

Reduce the risk of vulnerability to disasters that affect the university population. Considering that Arequipa is a seismic zone and that the University’s real estate heritage is old and not less than fifty years old, it is a peremptory requirement to have prevention, risk management and disaster response plan and system. There are plans for rehabilitation and planned reconstruction in the event of emergencies or natural disasters. Improve institutional management. Within the framework of the pillars of the modernization of Public Management, the purpose is to develop and consolidate a quality assurance system in all the University bodies and implement the integrated institutional management system with a process approach, which will result in a more efficient service in favour of the academic community. The strengthening of the accountability mechanism, implemented for periodic information to the university community, reinforces democracy and transparency. The better positioning of the University in the national and international academic community will generate greater opportunities for students and professors.



The University campus is made up of three academic areas: Social, Engineering and Biomedical, each one implemented with libraries, documentation centres and laboratories; an administrative area; a cultural and artistic expressions area; and academic services such as a Language Centre, am University Institute of Sports, a Computer Centre, and others. The udep has about 34 premises (7 for academic training and 27 for administrative and extracurricular activities, and production or experimental facilities), 18 investment projects in infrastructure, 171 laboratories and 84 workshops. Among all this infrastructure that is available to its students and academic community, it is worth highlighting the udep Museum, the Sumbay Yanahuara Experimental Centre, the Technological Research and Training Institute (INICITE), the Mineral Segregation and Foundry Laboratories, the TV udep Broadcast Centre, the Pedro Paulet Geophysical Institute and Aerospace Astronomical Institute, the Teaching Hospital, a Seed Processing Plant, the Majes Experimental Center, the Hydrobiological Research and Development Institute, among others.


The udep is among the nine Peruvian universities with the highest worldwide visibility with more than 290 works published in journals indexed in Scopus, according to the SCImago Ranking in the 2014 – 2018 quinquennium. In 2019 it received the ELSEVIER – CONCYTEC Award for having more than 600 scientific articles indexed during the period 2014 – 2018, in the southern region of Peru. Currently, 165 basic and applied research projects are being developed. Nationwide, it is in second place in the INDECOPI (National Institute for the Defense of Competition and the Protection of Intellectual Property) Ranking of patent applications, being the first as a Public University. Additionally, the udep is among the five Peruvian universities with a license from SUNEDU (National Superintendency of Higher University Education) for a maximum term of 10 years.

The objective of the incubator is to provide advice and support to students and the Southern Macro Region community on pre-incubation, incubation and acceleration issues.

UNIVERSITY DEPARTMENT FOR INNOVATION, DEVELOPMENT AND TECHNOLOGY TRANSFER - udep


The University Department for Innovation, Development and Technology Transfer is a line body of the Office of the Vice President for Research, in charge of promoting and managing relations and interactions between the University and society, in its different sectors, in the area of research and technological innovation.


The functions of the University Department for Innovation, Development and Technology Transfer are listed below :


    Promote, organize and manage Innovation Programs, Technological Development Programs and Agreements for technology transfer.

    Promote and manage relations between the University and society represented by companies, communities, scientific and cultural institutions, public or privates and others in the area of science and technology, specifically through research agreements, provision of services and consultancies.

    Promote the periodic holding of scientific, technological and innovation events, as well as social and biomedical sciences.



ENTREPRENEURSHIP DEVELOPMENT OFFICE - udep


The Entrepreneurship Development Office is an organic unit of the line of the University Office for Production of Goods and Services which is a support body for the Office of the President, in charge of conducting, guiding, controlling and evaluating the activities of the Centres and Units of Production of Goods and Services, in addition to promoting entrepreneurship at the University.


The functions of the Entrepreneurship Development Office are listed below :


    Create and implement the Business Incubator System at the University to accelerate the process of creation, growth and innovation and consolidation of companies, based on the entrepreneurial capacities of students and graduates to interrelate University, State, company and society.

    Create support environments through catalyst programs, linking students and professors with research centres, research institutes and centres for the production of goods and services to encourage the culture of research, entrepreneurship and innovation according to the Fourth Axis: For Research of the Statute of the udep.

    Transform the results of research into ventures, forming strategic alliances with research centres, networks of incubators and strategic partners at the level of financing, market and know-how. Define potential entrepreneurial sectors and markets to address their problems based on a mapping of the needs of the companies that comprise them and the stakeholders.

    Generate support systems for entrepreneurial ideas through networking programs (mentor networks), financing (angel investors, risk investments and innovation funds). Promote entrepreneurship and innovation in undergraduate and graduate students at the University.
